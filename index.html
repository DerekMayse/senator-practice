Ohio’s 6th Congressional District

Sherrod Brown

Brown is the senior senator from Ohio and is a Democrat. He has served since Jan 4, 2007. Brown is next up for reelection in 2024.
He was previously the representative for Ohio’s 13th congressional district as a Democrat from 1993 to 2006.

Senate Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
Senate Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry

Robert “Rob” Portman

Portman is the junior senator from Ohio and is a Republican. He has served since Jan 5, 2011. Portman is next up for reelection in 2022.
He was previously the representative for Ohio’s 2nd congressional district as a Republican from 1993 to 2005.
